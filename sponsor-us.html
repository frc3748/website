A Big THANK YOU to our sponsors!

Without the generosity of our sponsors and donors, we would not have the opportunity to provide enriching STEM experiences to students all across Howard County Public Schools (HCPSS). By sponsoring Team 3748 Ragnarok Robotics, you not only help a local team succeed, but also support the next generation of inventors, problem-solvers, and engineers. Your donation helps in purchasing robotics parts and tools, registering for competitions, and funding outreach programs.

The Gene Haas Foundation, established in 1999 by Gene Haas, the founder and owner of Haas Automation, Inc., is dedicated to supporting the local community through grants to various charities. Initially focused on local organizations such as the Boys and Girls Clubs, Food Share, and the Rescue Mission, the foundation recognized a growing need for skilled manufacturing employees globally. As a result, it expanded its mission to include support for manufacturing training programs across North America and beyond. Through scholarship grants, sponsorship of CNC competitions, and partnerships with top CNC training programs worldwide, the Gene Haas Foundation plays a crucial role in enhancing the availability of high-quality manufacturing technology education.
